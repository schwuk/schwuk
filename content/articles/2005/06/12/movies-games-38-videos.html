After a brief no-gaming spell, I find myself playing games once again. My CoH trial has expired, but I’m pretty sure I’m going to subscribe. I really want to try out Guild Wars as well, but this weekend I’ve picked up the game I’ve been waiting for… Grand Theft Auto : San Andreas. Although I’ve still got my PS2, I rarely play games on it now so I waited for the Xbox version to be released before picking it up. I’ve been a fan of GTA since the beginning, and I can safely say they’ve done it again.

Last night Mrs. Schwuk and I went to see Mr. & Mrs. Smith with some friends and as with GTA:SA I can thoroughly recommend it. Slow in places, but well worth watching.

Recently – thanks to Sky (One & Mix), E4 and Paramount Comedy Channel – I’ve gotten heavily into “Scrubs”. Fantastic series on so many levels. I’m (im)patiently awaiting the boxed sets to be released…
