A new LiveCD has just been released – mono::live 1.0. Based on Ubuntu Hoary, it includes everything you need to get a taste of Mono:

  • Mono 1.1.7
  • Various applications including F-Spot, Beagle, Tomboy and Muine
  • ASP.NET demos
  • MonoDevelop IDE
