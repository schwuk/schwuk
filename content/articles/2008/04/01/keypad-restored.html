--- type: page created_at: 2008-04-01 10:28:03 +01:00 title: Keypad Restored tags: - gnome - keyboard - keypad - Linux - Ubuntu --- After upgrading to Hardy I noticed that my numeric keypad was no longer working. Not a huge inconvenience, but when you've got one you like to be able to use it don't you? This morning - after trying to use it again - I decided to have another go at fixing it. A quick Google provided me with the solution:
  1. Go into the Keyboard Preferences (System >Preferences > Keyboard)
  2. Switch to the Mouse Keys tab
  3. Uncheck the box for Allow to control the pointer using the keyboard
Grammar excepted, a logical procedure but why (oh why) is it enabled by default?
