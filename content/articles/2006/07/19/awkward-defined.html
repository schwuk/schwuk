awk·ward adj.

1. Marked by or causing embarrassment or discomfort: an awkward remark; an awkward silence 2. Requiring great tact, ingenuity, skill, and discretion: An awkward situation arose during the peace talks 3. Your ex-boss returning to work after a leave of absence to be informed that they now work for you
