So I thought I’d celebrate the release of Ubuntu 6.06 (Dapper Drake”) by installing Microsoft Windows Vista Beta 2.

Before I get flamed I’d like to point out that I am running Dapper on another machine.

Initial impressions are:

  • Everything is nice and shiny
  • The install is pretty straight forward, and there’s are basically no options beside choosing to upgrade or install, and how you want to use the disks
  • For once all my hardware worked, although the soundcard needed drivers but Vista automatically retrieved them
  • It asks you to confirm most actions1 – this is A Good Thing™ on the whole but it can get annoying, especially when it’s asking you to confirm the action you just manually chosen!
  • IE7+ feels much like IE7 does

I’ll post some more when I’ve had time to delve into it further.
