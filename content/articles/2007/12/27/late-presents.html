I’ve deliberated over whether to get iPhone in the past. On Christmas Eve I actually went into an o2 shop with the intention of getting one, but something stopped me – I suspect it was the price and overpriced (and over long) contracts.

Yesterday my phone charger died a horrible and painful death, and although I could just have bought a new charger I instead rang o2 to see about an upgrade. Tomorrow I should have a shiny new Nokia N95 complete with 8GB card to play with.

Of course I’m not satisfied with just one new toy… My friend Mat told about the Nokia N800 being available for £149.99 on play.com before Christmas but I resisted. This morning I checked the site again and they were £129.99, and I just couldn’t resist this time. I know the N810 is out, but I’m not that bothered about a built-in keyboard (right now), I’ve got a GPS in the N95, and there’s just no comparison in price.

It looks like I’ve going to be uber-connected from now on!
